FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

01 What is VEXORA '26?

VEXORA '26 is a 24-hour national-level hackathon focused on solving real-world industrial challenges through rapid prototyping and code innovation.

02 Who can participate?

Registration is open to all undergraduate and postgraduate students. Teams of 2-4 members are eligible to enter the main hackathon track.

03 What should participants bring?

Participants must bring their own laptops, chargers, college ID, and any required hardware components.

04 How will projects be evaluated?

Projects will be judged based on innovation, technical implementation, problem-solving approach, and presentation.

05 Will participants receive certificates?

Yes. All participants will receive participation certificates, and winners will receive merit certificates and prizes.
